Title

Title is used if you wish others to know your work, professional, marital, or gender title. If you are a CEO of a network company, but you go by "Doctor" because you have a PhD, then you may wish to put down "Dr." or "Doctor" for your title. Other possible examples include Mrs., Ms. Mr. Sr., Sra, Srta, System Engineer, Consultant, Professor, etc.

Your title only appears if someone is looking for other potential workshop instructors or organizers and they click on your profile for detailed information.
