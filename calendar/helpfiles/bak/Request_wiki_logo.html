Wiki logo URL

If you have a logo that you would like to appear on the upper-right of each page in your wiki, then please provide the URL link to this logo. For example, the URL for the ISOC Workshop Resource Centre logo is "http://ws.edu.isoc.org/images/isoc_small.gif".

We will download your logo locally for use in your wiki. Ideally your logo should not be so large as to take up too much space on each page. Here are two examples of wikis that use logos on each page:
