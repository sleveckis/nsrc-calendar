ISOC Workshop Portal Privacy Policy

Information you enter in the ISOC Workshop Resource Centre will not be shared with any third parties outside of this web site. Other members of this site can see information that is given here for purposes of workshop collaboration.

Any registered user who distributes or sells any other member information for purposes other than workshop collaboration may have their account and use privileges removed.

Notes:

  1. If you wish to change your userid please contact administrative staff via email at admin@workshops.isoc.org. You will need to specify why the userid needs to be changed and verify your account information.
  2. Other userid information, including your password, can be changed via the ISOC Workshop Resource Centre web site.
