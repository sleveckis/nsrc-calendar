Search for Resource People by Travel or Remote Help

When a user registers on this site they indicate their willingness to do one, two, or all of the following three items:
  1. Willing or able to travel to teach or help with workshops.
  2. Remotely support via email or phone preparation of teaching materials.
  3. Remotely support via email or phone coordination and organization of workshop logistics.
With this search you can find users who have indicated that are willing to help with workshop logistics in one, or more, of these manners.
