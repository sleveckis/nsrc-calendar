Search for Resource People by Travel or Remote Help

When a user registers on this site they can indicate a number of areas of expertise. In addition, based on the country where they live they are assigned to their region. This search allows you to find registered users by expertise, by whether they list themselves as Organizer, Speaker, or Instructor, what zone they live in, and any languages they may have listed as knowing.

Belowing is a summary of each of the items list on this search form in the order they are listed:

  1. Language or languages spoken. This will search on Primary, Secondary, and Terciary languages listed.
  2. Region where the person lives based on the country information they filled in.
  3. Workshop organizer
  4. Workshop presenter
  5. Lab-based workshop instructor
  6. Routing and network infrastructure instructor
  7. IP services instructor
  8. Applications and Help Desk activities instructor
  9. Wireless technology instructor
The more items you pick the more specific this search becomes and fewer results will be returned.

You can choose any individual item and performance a complete user search on that singled item.
